"Parks and Recreation" is a mockumentary-style sitcom that aired from 2009 to 2015, set in the fictional town of Pawnee, Indiana. The series follows the daily operations and antics of the town's Parks and Recreation Department, led by the ever-enthusiastic and optimistic Leslie Knope (played by Amy Poehler). Throughout its seven seasons, the show delves into the personal and professional lives of its diverse cast of characters, including the stoic Ron Swanson, the health-obsessed Chris Traeger, the sarcastic April Ludgate, and the entrepreneurial Tom Haverford, among others.
